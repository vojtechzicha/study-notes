Logic

The notation for sequent \Gamma = B_{1},\ldots,B_{n} \vdash A means that the sequence \Gamma of propositions B_{1},..,B_{n} proved the conclusion A. Logic uses several logical connective – conjunction,

We have several logical postures: assumptions of sequents, introduction of conjunction \land_{i} and elimination of conjunction \land_{e}.
